Detailed Description

template<typename PointT>
class pcl::VoxelGridCovariance< PointT >

A searchable voxel strucure containing the mean and covariance of the data.

Note
For more information please see Magnusson, M. (2009). The Three-Dimensional Normal-Distributions Transform — an Efﬁcient Representation for Registration, Surface Analysis, and Loop Detection. PhD thesis, Orebro University. Orebro Studies in Technology 36
Author
Brian Okorn (Space and Naval Warfare Systems Center Pacific)
